The Big Sas

The Man!

The Sasquach also known as Sasquachium biggus footious, is a species native to the PNW(pacific northwest) and is known to get angry if messed with.

A quote from wikipedia

"The enormous footprints for which the creature is named are claimed to be as large as 24 inches (60 cm) long and 8 inches (20 cm) wide."
